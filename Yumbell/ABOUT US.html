Yumbell Donado Maury is the owner of the firm bearing his name initials. Prior to starting the firm Yumbell was a Senior Architectural Designer at Saks Fifth Avenue of Store Planning, Design & Visual Presentation. Yumbell held various positions at Macy's, as Store Planner and Designer. Overseeing Macy's East 800 Stores.

Yumbell's 36 year retail background includes Construction, Design, Visual, and Integrating brands within a brand. He has been an Architectural Consultant for Estee Lauder, Nautica, Michael Kors, Bergdorf and Goodman Stores and Design Firms like Walker Group, TSR and FRCH. He is recognized as a leader with in the retail industry.

The YDM Design roster of international clients includes retail projects, residential design and construction project management in Kazakhstan, France, Brazil, Puerto Rico, Mexico, Colombia, Ecuador, Panama, Italy, India, United Arab Emirates, Canada, Singapore, Bahrain, Spain, Netherlands, China, Australia and the United States.

Yumbell Donado Maury received his education at the Corporacion Universitaria de la Costa Barranquilla Colombia, NYC Technical College, City College of the City University of New York and Fashion Institute of Technology.
