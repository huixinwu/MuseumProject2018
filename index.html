Create Your Smartphone App Workshop
This workshop will guide you to understand the technology behind the applications for smartphones. It will teach you how to create simple applications for mobile phones, what are the tools and the free software to create an application web, the basic steps to design a web page application, understanding how to complete and publish a smartphone application.

Participants will work on creating their own phone application and their experiences with the Queens Museum and the New New Yorkers program.

Workshop Instructor
Huixin(Maritza) Wu earned her Master in Electrical Engineering from Stony Brook University. She is also a network and website design certified from Stony Brook University and Queens College.
Ms. Wu has been an instructor at both New York City College of Technology, NYCCT, and Queensborough Community College since 2007, and she is currently a technology engineering lecturer at QCC.

“It was a pleasure serving my community and teaching people excited to learn in the field of technology. I learned a lot from my students and their passion and effort to carry out a technological project. I am very happy with the results, and I want to congratulate all the participants and thanks to the New New Yorkers program for making this workshop possible” Maritza Wu
